From humble beginnings.

As a kid growing up in the city of Detroit, I was enamoured by the Tigers. I would try to sneak in, or pay my way into any game I could, and when I couldn't, I would watch at the barber shop up the road that was owned by a family friend. One day, as he swept the hair out his front door and into the street, I noticed something. A bird was picking up some of the hair and using it as nesting material for its eggs.
This incedent had a profound effect on me. The idea that birds were having to resort to using human waste to survive was an idea that made me feel disgusted. We had a hair problem, and no one wanted to talk about it. I knew it was time to step up and speak my truth. The HHTBP isn't just about selling a superior product, it's also about getting serious when it comes to taking care of the planet.
